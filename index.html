ABOUT ME

I hold the Master of Public Health degree at Cornell and the Bachelor degree at Fudan University, majoring in Pharmaceutical Science.
I have broad experiences in biomedical research, such as molecular biology, virology and biochemistry. My projects have covered cerebral disease microenvironment studies, breast cancer studies, and coronavirus studies. My goal is to build a life-long research career in a leading research institute and reduce the disease impact on humans by fundamental research.
I am particularly interested in virus-host cell interactions, viral structure functions, and imaging technologies both in vitro and in vivo.
I also enjoy creating scientific illustrations to help better understand the science.
